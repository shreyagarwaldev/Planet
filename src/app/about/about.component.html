About Us

PixelatedPlanet enables aspiring photographers to find workshops and seminars all around the world being conducted by people successful in the field of photography. On the other hand, we provide a platform for photographers to showcase their photography workshops and reach interested folks.

FAQs

Do you have workshops of all genres?
Of course! You can find everything from Landscape and Food photography to Timelapse and Night Sky photography workshops.

Does PixelatedPlanet allow me to register for workshops?
As of now, we aim to bring all photography workshops in one place. After finding the workshop you like, we will redirect you the photographer's website for registration/payment.

Does PixelatedPlanet charge me for finding workshops?
No. All information on our website if freely accessible. No hidden costs.

I am a photographer. Can I showcase my workshops on your website?
We carefully select photographers based on their portfolios to keep quality of workshops high. Please email us at hello@thepixelatedplanet.com in order to get in touch with us!
